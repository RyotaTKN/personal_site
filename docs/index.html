ABOUT ME

I am a PhD candidate majoring social psychology, cognitive psychology, and neuroscience at Kyoto University.

I use multiple methodologies that include behavioral experiments, neuroimaging, and psychophysiology to understand the hierarchical (emotional, cognitive, social) mechanisms underlying how humans perceive themselves, others, groups, and the world.
