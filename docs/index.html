ABOUT ME

I am a PhD candidate majoring social psychology, cognitive psychology, and neuroscience at Kyoto University.

I use multiple methodologies that include behavioral experiments, physiological measurement, and neuroimaging (mind, body, and brain) to understand the hierarchical (emotional, cognitive, social) mechanisms underlying how humans perceive themselves, others, groups, and the world.

E-mail: takano.ryota.43a[at]st.kyoto-u.ac.jp
